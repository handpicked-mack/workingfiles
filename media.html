The Art of Curation

The Handpicked story grew from the simple, yet bold desire to bring the world of wine under one label. It has since evolved to encompass a world of wine, focused entirely upon the concept of regionality, curation and travel. We call it, the Art of Curation.

The Art of Curation tells the story of Handpicked’s Collection series – a set of premium wines from various hand-picked regions – each crafted to reflect a supreme sense of place. For these wines quality and care is not only applied to grape growing and winemaking but also to packaging. Each label features a unique object collected and photographed from its region. These objects not only capture the essence of traditional ‘terroir’ but also the broader environmental factors that come together to give each wine its distinctive personality and taste.

The Art of Curation represents a metaphor for Handpicked’s wine-making process – the act of taking something natural and turning it into something incredible. A grape is grown from nature but transformed through care into extraordinary wine. Similarly, the objects represented within the series are ordinary objects which have been taken from nature, curated and transformed.

Gold is the unifying element which ties the objects together – it is the ‘Handpicked’ touch.

So what does The Art of Curation mean for you? Ultimately, it is an invitation to you, the drinker, to ‘travel the world, one wine at a time.’

Handpicked Wine Wedding Melbourne

A wine wedding is just like a regular wedding without all the boring bits - no long speeches, weird aunts or expensive gifts required. Just good fashion, fine wine and music in an amazing location.

Held on the rooftop at Notel Melbourne​, each caravan was decked out to a different theme: Buck's Party, Honeymoon Suite, Newlyweds, Odd Relatives and Bridesmaids. Wines included Prosecco, Pinot Grigio, Riesling, Chardonnay, Rosé and Pinot Noir.
